Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ShortHaul services.

How do I pay for my ride?

You can pay for your ride using multiple methods: cash, UPI payments, or through the app using your credit/debit card. All payments are secure and encrypted.

Is there an option for female drivers?

Yes! We offer a "Female Rider" option that allows you to specifically request a female driver for your ride. This option is available for bike taxis and auto rides.

How does Toto Pooling work?

Toto Pooling matches you with other riders going in the same direction. You share the ride and split the fare, making it more affordable for everyone while still maintaining comfort.

What safety features do you offer?

Our safety features include real-time ride tracking, SOS button, driver verification, ride sharing with emergency contacts, and the option to choose female drivers.
